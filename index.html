About Us

We are a small game development company with people all around the world working with us. We are a fun community project that we hope you will enjoy.

Where else can you go to work and spend the day drawing, dreaming, and playing video games? Here at Gregplant Development we encourage our team members to do just that.

And that's what keeps us loving what we do, and making really great games. Gregplant Development strives to be the best possible game studio it can be. Bringing their imagination and vision to life. Get in touch with us to learn more about our projects.
